WHY?

Whilst Broadband is widely available and provides a relatively inexpensive way to connect, say retail outlets to a payment provider or Branch offices to a Head office, it's not the most reliable connection type since most broadband services are currently being delivered on a 97% or worse availability guarantee.

What that means is that your broadband connection is, on average, likely to be unavailable for more than 10 days every year. If you are a retailer imagine if that 10 days is just before Xmas, how much business would you lose if you couldn't accept card payments? Or if you are a Small business owner imagine if you couldn't send or receive email for 10 days? Before TFM's High availability Broadband solution you would have to upgrade to a dedicated Ethernet solution to get close to the kind of availability guarantees (99% on average for Ethernet) you really need as a business, but that comes at a significant price increase in most cases at least 5 times the cost of normal Broadband.

SO HOW DO WE DO IT?

We have bundled our normal Business Copper or Fibre Broadband services together with our unique automatic 4G failover solution. Our 4G services are delivered on our on Private APN from the 2 main UK providers directly into our Core network so failover is absolutely seamless and security is never compromised as it is truly ON-Net. The services are terminated on specialist hardware solution which supports, not only Broadband and 4G but also Ethernet, WiFi and VoIP in a single unit meaning you can add additional services without the need for upgrade. Together this package allows us to deliver a 99.99% availability guarantee a first in the UK.
